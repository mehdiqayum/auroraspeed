Who we are

We are Aurora Speed, your premier partner in logistics and cargo services in Afghanistan and around the globe. We specialize in delivering efficient, reliable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to meet your unique business needs. Our dedicated team ensures that your goods reach their destination safely and on time, empowering your business to thrive in an ever-competitive marketplace. Our mission is to provide innovative logistics and cargo solutions that enhance our clients' operations and contribute to their success. Our vision is to become the leading logistics provider in Afghanistan and beyond, recognized for our exceptional service, reliability, and customer satisfaction. Our goals include optimizing supply chain efficiency, reducing costs, and delivering unparalleled support to our clients. With years of experience in supply chain management, cargo handling, and IT services, we are committed to understanding the unique needs of each client and delivering tailored solutions that drive results.

How It Works

i

How Supply Works

  • Submit your request through our online portal or contact our support team.
  • We will review your requirements and provide a customized quote based on your needs.
  • Once you approve the quote, we will initiate the supply process, coordinating with suppliers and managing logistics.
ii

How Cargo Services Work

  • Request a quote for your cargo shipment by providing details about the goods and destination.
  • Our team will prepare a competitive shipping quote, including transit times and options.
  • Upon acceptance, we will manage the entire shipment process, from pickup to delivery, keeping you updated throughout.
iii

How IT Services Work

  • Discuss your IT needs with our consultants, who will assess your current systems and requirements.
  • Receive a tailored proposal that outlines our recommended IT solutions.
  • Upon approval, our team will implement the solutions, providing ongoing support to ensure seamless integration.
